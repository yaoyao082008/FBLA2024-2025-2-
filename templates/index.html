Finance Calculator App

This program allows users to track their finances and generate earnings and graphs. First the user must sign in with an google account. Then the app allows users to input their income types and expense types including amount, catergory, and date of each transactions. Afterwards, it allows user to calculate their total balance. The user can update or delete their income or expenses accordingly and also filter their inputs. Finally, the app analyzes the spending patterns by displaying 3 graphs : End of month balance, Net Income Charts, Savings Calculator, Income type, Expense Type.

To add a class, click the green add left of the income header. Then select the type of income and the date.

To remove an Income and or Expense, click the red remove button next to the right of the class.

To analyze your spending data simply click the green analyze button on the bottom of the page. Then you will be redirected to 3 graphs that you can analyze.

Use our AI chatbot at the bottom right for any extra inquiries !
